Diogenes

The Man Who Trolled Plato

In one famous anecdote, Plato attempted to characterize humanity using the definition of his teacher Socrates. Humans were, in his words, “featherless bipeds.” On hearing the news, Diogenese brought a plucked chicken to the Academy, announcing he had found Plato’s human. Diogenes won the argument. The Academics were forced to revise their definition of a human, albeit to a ‘flat-nailed featherless biped‘.
